What We Do.
Founded in 2022, Psych Studios is an independent game development studio. We are currently working on mods for popular visual novel "Doki Doki Literature Club!". We believe that all our content should be of the highest quality possible, and stirve to ensure that at all times.
Yuri Reality.
Yuri Reality is a fan mod for Doki Doki Literature Club!, set in an alternate universe at the same time as the original game.
